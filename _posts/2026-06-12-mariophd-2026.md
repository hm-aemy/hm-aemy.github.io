Congratulations to Dr. Mario Qosja who successfully defended his PhD thesis
titled "Time Triggered Organic Computing" on June 10th at the University of
Siegen.

In his thesis, Dr. Qosja combined Organic Computing (OC) in the form of
Artificial DNA and Artificial Hormone Systems with time triggered communication
networks and created the Time-Triggered Organic Computing architecture which
allows for the design and implementation of reliable, adaptive and safe
real-time embedded systems.
