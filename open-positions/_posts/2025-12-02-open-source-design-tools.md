We offer positions in our team for current and upcoming projects in the area of open source chip design tools.

Your tasks include:

- Analysis of necessary transformations between chip design tools
- Prototypical implementation of transformations between established open-source chip design tools
- Active contribution to the open-source framework CIRCT for digital circuit design

You are the excellent candidate, if you fullfill the following requirements

- Completed Master's degree in Computer Science, Electrical and Information Engineering, or comparable fields of study
- Basic understanding of compilers
- Solid knowledge of software development in C++
- Previous open-source activities desirable
